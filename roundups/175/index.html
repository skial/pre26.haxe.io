# Haxe Roundup № 175

Ido has released on bitbucket his Pixi.js extern's so you can get all the awesomeness from using Haxe on top of Pixi.js!

FlashDevelop 4.5 is out with some major improvements around Haxe and OpenFL support. Love it.

Kha, the OpenFL alternative, now has a google+ community page. Go and join! Their documentation is slowly coming together.

Jason has written a Super Quick Haxe Abstracts Example which demonstrates their simple side. When your ready, take a look at some of the more advanced features Haxe abstracts have to offer. Be careful as it's far too easy to abuse their features.

And talking of advanced abstract features, how about a tutorial on multi-type abstracts by Dan.

Alexander has posted a short post on how to fix the OpenFL 1.1 linux error Could not initialize SDL : No available video device.

The first, new, Haxe Hangout took place last Thursday in the Haxe irc channel. You can read the irc logs to keep up. Just search for [16:01:01] <fponticelli> it is time! :) to find the start.

And let's finish with that Adrian has published the source code for Pole Vaultage!, a frustratingly good game, to github.
